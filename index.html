Rush hour delays as snow returns to parts of Scotland

Commuters have faced delays after snow showers returned to some parts of Scotland. Delays were reported in the central belt on Tuesday morning, including routes around Edinburgh, the M77 and the M80. Met Office yellow warnings for snow and ice remain in place for large parts of the country until 21:00 on Tuesday.
